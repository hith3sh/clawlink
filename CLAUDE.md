@AGENTS.md

ClawLink is a SaaS for OpenClaw users that makes integrations drastically easier to connect than manual setup. Product and UX decisions should prioritize simplicity, fewer steps, hosted flows, and language that non-technical users can understand quickly.

Business direction: first integration free, then a paid subscription model around `$5/month`, with a roadmap toward 40+ integrations that help OpenClaw users do useful work.

For OpenClaw plugin releases, follow the tag-based npm publish workflow documented in `AGENTS.md` and `.github/workflows/publish-openclaw-plugin.yml`.
