## Automation Tips

**1. Write once, publish everywhere**
- Write your post on your own blog or in a Google Doc.
- Use **Make.com** or **Zapier** to auto-cross-post to Medium, Dev.to, and Hashnode. Many of these platforms have APIs or RSS import features.

**2. Repurpose with AI**
- Use an AI tool (or a simple prompt pipeline) to turn one long blog post into:
  - A LinkedIn article (shorter, more personal)
  - A Reddit post (question/discussion format)
  - A Twitter/X thread (key takeaways)
  - An Indie Hackers story (founder narrative)

**3. Schedule in batches**
- Use **Buffer**, **Hypefury**, or **Typefully** to queue posts across LinkedIn, Twitter, and Reddit so you're not manually posting every day.

**4. RSS - Medium/Dev.to**
- Both Medium and Dev.to can import from an RSS feed. If you publish on your own site, you can set up automatic syndication.

**5. GitHub awesome-list submissions**
- These are one-time PRs, but you can script a reminder or keep a checklist to submit ClawLink to new lists as they appear.
